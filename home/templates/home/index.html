We are #GameChangers

Traditional training emphasises commands and cues, like ‘sit’ ‘down’ and ‘stay’. But our methods take a different approach – one backed by the latest science. We teach dogs to think and make good choices, and we encourage them to work with you.

I’m an active member of the Pro Dog Trainer community, and regularly attend training workshops and seminars run by the world-renowned vet behaviourist, Tom Mitchell, and Crufts agility winner, Lauren Langman.

The Pro Dog Trainer Method doesn’t begin training with cues like ‘sit’ ‘down’ and ‘stay’. It is not the most important skill to start teaching your puppy or adult dog and it’s impractical, as it doesn’t simulate your dog’s day-to-day life. Knowing how to ‘sit’ doesn’t help a dog to come back when called, or restrain themselves when there’s wildlife to chase, noises to bark at, and ankles to bite.

Instead, we work through fun games which shape your dog’s brain – and their behaviour. We give them skills such as focus, confidence and calmness.

Whatever training or behavioural struggle you are facing, just remember "There’s a game for that!"
